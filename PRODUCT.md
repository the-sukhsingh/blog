# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Content editors and publishers managing posts, along with general readers visiting the public site.

## Product Purpose
Providing a distraction-free, highly customized content publishing workspace and reader interface.

## Positioning
Ultra-lightweight, customizable rich-text publishing with no boilerplate or plugin bloat.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, focused, and professional. Inspired by the clean, high-density structures found in modern developer tools and publishing platforms.

## Anti-references
WordPress/standard templates characterized by clunky layouts, bloated sidebars, and generic styling templates.

## Design Principles
- **Focus Over Friction**: Prioritize a clean, quiet workspace and interface that highlights content and structure above all else.
- **Expert Confidence**: Present tools and layouts with clean lines and structural clarity, conveying professional capability and reliable execution.
- **Frictionless Flow**: Emphasize smooth, instant interactions for editors writing rich content and readers seeking information.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA compliance (contrast ratio ≥4.5:1, screen reader support, keyboard navigability, respects prefers-reduced-motion).
